Kaitiaki Quest 🌿

Plastic

Only some plastics can be recycled.

Only recycle plastics with numbers 1, 2, or 5.

Rinse bottles and containers.

Remove lids before recycling.

No plastic bags or soft plastics.

Paper

Paper and cardboard can be recycled.

Flatten cardboard boxes.

Keep paper clean and dry.

Newspapers, magazines and boxes are OK.

No wet or dirty paper.

Food Waste

Food scraps go in food waste bins.

Fruit and veggie scraps are good.

Leftover food can go here.

Can turn into compost.

Do not put food in recycling.

Landfill

Items that cannot be recycled go here.

Plastic bags and wrappers.

Broken items and dirty waste.

Coffee cups and nappies.

These items go to landfill.

Glass

Glass bottles and jars can be recycled.

Rinse glass before recycling.

Remove lids.

Only bottles and jars.

No broken glass or mirrors.

Fun Facts

  • Plastic can take hundreds of years to break down!
  • Recycling saves energy and helps the environment.
  • Sorting rubbish correctly helps protect animals.
